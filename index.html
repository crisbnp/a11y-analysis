Analysing the accessibility of Indonesian Embassy in the UK

By Cristien Natal

Motivation to analyse this website

As an Indonesian who lives abroad in the UK, I do go to the website when I need to renew passport or find relevant information. As it is a government website, I would expect an accessible website that caters towards all users. Unfortunately, I find it difficult to navigate the website as the hierachy of website is not clear and there are a lot of moving content which is hard to follow.

Accessibility Review plan:

I want to make sure the website is accessible for users who rely heavily on keyboard to navigate by choice or otherwise. Users with motor impairments such as RSI or paralysis as well as screen readers.
